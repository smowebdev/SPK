Stanford Knight is the only agent offering CIIP through the Vanuatu Impact Fund. Your capital is independently managed and secured under a capital preservation policy. Zero Client Fees

As a government-appointed Master Agent, SKP is paid directly by the Vanuatu government. The prices on this website are the complete, all-inclusive costs — government fees plus due diligence. There are no agent fees, no hidden charges, and no additions. This is a unique position in the market.

100% Lodged Success Rate — Backed by Free Pre-Screening

Every application Stanford Knight has lodged with the Citizenship Commission of Vanuatu has been approved. Not most. Every one. The reason is structural: Vanuatu’s process has an FIU pre-screening stage before formal lodgement, and we offer our own complimentary pre-screening before you commit a single fee. Issues are caught there, not at lodgement. The headline figure follows from the process.

Key Benefits

Why Vanuatu?

A premier citizenship destination with tangible, lifelong benefits.

Visa-Free Travel

Access 100+ countries visa-free, including Singapore, Hong Kong, and many others.

Zero Personal Income Tax

Vanuatu levies no personal income tax, no capital gains tax, no inheritance tax, and no wealth tax. A genuinely tax-efficient jurisdiction.

Crypto-Friendly Jurisdiction

No cryptocurrency taxation. Progressive digital asset regulation. Increasingly popular with digital asset holders and blockchain entrepreneurs.

Offshore Banking

Access to international banking relationships. Vanuatu's financial centre offers legitimate offshore structuring for global businesses.

Hereditary Citizenship

Citizenship passes to your children and their children. A multigenerational asset that grows in value over time.

Plan B Passport

Geopolitical insurance. A second citizenship provides options when circumstances change — for you and your family.

100% Remote Process

No travel to Vanuatu required. The entire application is managed remotely. (Biometric centre attendance required for passport issuance.) Passport delivered to your door.

Fast Processing

60 days on average from application to passport. One of the fastest citizenship programmes globally.
