Recommendation Logic

Which Programme
Is Right for You?

DSP
From: USD $135,000
(Single Applicant)

Choose DSP if:

  • You are a single applicant or small family seeking the lowest single-applicant entry
  • You prefer the lowest upfront cost ($135,000)
  • You do not need redemption or Impact Fund features
CIIP
From: USD $165,000
(Principal + spouse + two children)

Choose DSP if:

  • You are a couple or family with children under 18
  • You prefer the lower overall cost over time, with the return of your $50,000 plus any investment returns after 4 years
  • You value impact investment and measurable social outcomes
  • You want something no other agent can offer (exclusive to SKP)
