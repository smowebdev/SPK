Programme Overview

Citizenship and Capital, Combined

CIIP combines full Vanuatu citizenship for a family with a $50,000 investment in the Vanuatu Impact Fund — a sustainable development initiative supporting smallholder farmers across Vanuatu.

After four years, your $50,000 is returned in full. The effective cost of CIIP becomes $115,000 — making it the lowest net-cost pathway to Vanuatu citizenship for a qualifying family.

Stanford Knight & Partners is the exclusive Master Agent for the Vanuatu Impact Fund. The Fund was developed in direct partnership with the Vanuatu Government and is not available through any other provider.

Eligibility

Who Qualifies for DSP?

Eligibility requirements are identical to DSP. Additionally, CIIP applicants should be comfortable with a four-year investment commitment and aligned with the Impact Fund’s sustainable development objectives.

You are elibible if you:

  • Are 18 years of age or older
  • Have a legitimate, verifiable source of funds
  • Have a clean criminal record (no serious convictions)
  • Are in good financial standing (no bankruptcy, no outstanding judgements)
  • Can provide professional and financial references

Dependent family members:

  • Are 18 years of age or older
  • Have a legitimate, verifiable source of funds
  • Have a clean criminal record (no serious convictions)
  • Are in good financial standing (no bankruptcy, no outstanding judgements)
  • Can provide professional and financial references

You may face adicional assessment if:

  • Are 18 years of age or older
  • Have a legitimate, verifiable source of funds
  • Have a clean criminal record (no serious convictions)
  • Are in good financial standing (no bankruptcy, no outstanding judgements)
  • Can provide professional and financial references

You are likely ineligible if:

  • Are 18 years of age or older
  • Have a legitimate, verifiable source of funds
  • Have a clean criminal record (no serious convictions)
  • Are in good financial standing (no bankruptcy, no outstanding judgements)
  • Can provide professional and financial references
