Why our pricing is different

Most agents in this industry add their own fees on top of government costs — typically 10–20% of the total. Some disclose this upfront. Others build it into inflated price quotes that grow during the application. As a government-appointed Master Agent, Stanford Knight is paid directly by the Vanuatu Government. The price you see is the price you pay. There is no additional layer.

Choose DSP if:

you are a single applicant or small family seeking the lowest single-applicant entry point ($135,000), and you do not need the Impact Fund’s redemption feature.

Choose CIIP if:
