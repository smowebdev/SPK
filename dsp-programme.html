Programme Overview

The Principal Citizenship Pathway

The Development Support Programme is Vanuatu’s principal citizenship pathway. A single contribution of USD $135,000 provides full Vanuatu citizenship and a passport for the principal applicant. Spouses, children and dependent parents are added at published per-head fees during the application.

Average processing time is 60 days. The application is 100% remote — no travel required. (Biometric centre attendance is required for passport issuance.) Your citizenship is permanent, hereditary, and carries no ongoing fees or obligations.

Eligibility

Who Qualifies for DSP?

You are elibible if you:
  • Are 18 years of age or older
  • Have a legitimate, verifiable source of funds
  • Have a clean criminal record (no serious convictions)
  • Are in good financial standing (no bankruptcy, no outstanding judgements)
  • Can provide professional and financial references
Dependent family members:
  • Are 18 years of age or older
  • Have a legitimate, verifiable source of funds
  • Have a clean criminal record (no serious convictions)
  • Are in good financial standing (no bankruptcy, no outstanding judgements)
  • Can provide professional and financial references
You may face adicional assessment if:
  • Are 18 years of age or older
  • Have a legitimate, verifiable source of funds
  • Have a clean criminal record (no serious convictions)
  • Are in good financial standing (no bankruptcy, no outstanding judgements)
  • Can provide professional and financial references
You are likely ineligible if:
  • Are 18 years of age or older
  • Have a legitimate, verifiable source of funds
  • Have a clean criminal record (no serious convictions)
  • Are in good financial standing (no bankruptcy, no outstanding judgements)
  • Can provide professional and financial references
