The architecture

We Created the Legislation

Stanford Knight’s senior advisory team participated directly in the creation of Vanuatu’s citizenship by investment legislation in 2012, working alongside the Prime Minister’s office and the Citizenship Commission.

This is not marketing language. No other agent in the market — none — can make this claim.

What it means for you

You are working with people who understand the programme from its foundations — not from a brochure, but from the legislation itself.

we are insiders

Head Office: Port Vila, Vanuatu

Our head office is in Port Vila, directly adjacent to the Citizenship Commission of Vanuatu. We are not intermediaries. We are insiders.
We also maintain offices in Dubai and London for client convenience — but our operational centre is where it matters most.

What it means for you

Same-day government communication. Immediate response to queries. No time-zone delays. Your application is never waiting for an email to be returned.

Exclusivity

The Only Agent With Impact Fund Access

The Vanuatu Impact Fund is a sustainable development initiative supporting smallholder farmers and rural communities across Vanuatu. It is the investment vehicle for the CIIP programme — and it is exclusively available through Stanford Knight & Partners.

No other agent can offer CIIP through this fund. No other agent has access to the impact investment pathway. This is a proprietary programme developed by SKP in partnership with the Vanuatu government.

What it means for you

If you want citizenship that generates measurable social impact — with annual reporting, verified outcomes, and a $50,000 investment returned after four years — Stanford Knight is your only option.

cost

No Agent Fees. Ever.

As a government-appointed Master Agent, Stanford Knight is paid directly by the Vanuatu government. We do not charge clients any additional fees. The prices on this website are the complete, all-inclusive costs — government fees plus due diligence (and the redeemable Impact Fund contribution for CIIP). Nothing added.

Most agents in this industry add their own fees on top of government costs — typically 10–20% of the total. Some disclose this upfront. Others build it into inflated price quotes that only become clear during the application.

What it means for you

The price you see is the price you pay. There is no additional layer.

Our track record

100% on Applications Lodged with the Citizenship Commission

The reason is structural. We do not lodge applications we believe will fail.

Vanuatu’s process has an FIU (Financial Intelligence Unit) pre-screening stage before formal lodgement — a due-diligence check carried out before any government fee is paid. To protect clients, we offer a complimentary pre-screening of our own before you formally engage. We look at the same factors the FIU will:
