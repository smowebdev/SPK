DOCUMENT CHECKLIST

Exactly What You'll Need

Every document required for Vanuatu citizenship — for you, your family, and your application. No surprises. No last-minute requests.

Most clients already have around 80% of these. We tell you up front what you need — and we prepare the rest for you.

BEFORE YOU SCROLL

Why This List Looks Long

Vanuatu's programme is regulated, and the document set protects both you and the Citizenship Commission of Vanuatu. The list is comprehensive on purpose — but it is also predictable, and most of what is needed is already in your filing cabinet.

THREE THINGS ARE WORTH KNOWING BEFORE YOU SCROLL FURTHER:

01

You likely have 80% already

Most clients already have 80% of these documents. The remaining 20% are usually a police clearance, a medical certificate, and one or two certified copies — straightforward to obtain.

02

We prepare four key documents

Stanford Knight prepares four key documents for you. You sign and witness them; we do the drafting.

See the four below
03

Submitted only once

Documents are submitted only once. You will not be asked for the same thing twice. Our preparation process eliminates the back-and-forth that delays applications elsewhere.
