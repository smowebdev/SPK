How it works

How the partnership works

Every stage is handled by your SKP team. The points where payment falls due are marked, so you always know what is owed and when.

Initial conversation

Confidential, NDA-backed where appropriate, to understand your client base, geography, volume, and the kind of partnership that fits.

Formal agreement

A clear, plain-English partner agreement covering scope, commercials, communication protocols, and reporting. Governed by Vanuatu law. No market exclusivity is granted, but every partner is treated as a long-term relationship.

Onboarding

Relationship manager assigned, partner pack delivered (process documentation, programme briefs, client materials, document checklists), and a kick-off call to align on referral or sub-agent workflow.

Live referrals and applications

Your clients receive Stanford Knight’s full service, including free pre-screening, document preparation, and end-to-end management to passport.

Quarterly reviews

Performance, pipeline, regulatory updates, and any joint marketing activity.
