After approval

From Approval to Passport in Hand

Approval is the milestone. The passport is the deliverable. Here is what happens between the two — managed end to end by your SKP team.

01

Civil status registration

Your citizenship certificate is issued by the Citizenship Commission. We then complete your civil status registration, which generates your Vanuatu birth registration certificate and national ID card. We hold the originals on file and provide certified copies for your immediate use.

02

Biometric enrolment booking

Vanuatu’s passport is biometric and ICAO-compliant. Manual passports are no longer issued, so every applicant — including children — must attend an in-person enrolment appointment. We submit your biometrics registration to the Vanuatu Passport Office on your behalf, alongside the supporting documents (citizenship certificate, civil status certificates, FIU clearance, police clearance, and a copy of your original passport), and book your appointment at the location best suited to you.

03

In-person biometric appointment

You attend in person to provide fingerprints, a passport photograph, and facial recognition data.

04

Passport printed, couriered, delivered

Your passport is printed in Vanuatu, collected by our team, and delivered to your nominated address by secure courier. From biometric appointment to passport in hand is typically a further 2–3 weeks.
